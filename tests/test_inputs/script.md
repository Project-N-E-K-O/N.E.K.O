# Test Input Recording Script

Please record the following lines clearly.
Save each file as `[ID].wav` in the `tests/test_inputs/` directory.

## Voice Loop Test (10 turns)
These will be played sequentially in the voice loop test.

1. **[001.wav]** Hello, are you there?
2. **[002.wav]** Can you tell me what time it is?
3. **[003.wav]** What is the weather like today?
4. **[004.wav]** Tell me a short joke.
5. **[005.wav]** Sing a little song for me.
6. **[006.wav]** Who is your favorite character?
7. **[007.wav]** I am testing your voice recognition.
8. **[008.wav]** Can you hear me clearly?
9. **[009.wav]** Let's talk about something else.
10. **[010.wav]** Goodbye for now.

## Video Call Test
These lines will be used when testing video/vision capabilities.

11. **[011.wav]** Look at my screen, what do you see?
12. **[012.wav]** Can you describe the image I'm showing you?

## General/Interrupt Test
Used for random interjections or testing interruption (if needed later).

13. **[013.wav]** Wait, stop for a second.
